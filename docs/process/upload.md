# Upload to the TPR-DB

Once you create an account on the [CRITT TPR-DB Web Application](https://critt.as.kent.edu/tpr/), from your dashboard, click on “My Studies” and then “Create Study”. From there, follow all the prompts to upload keystroke logging files. The first step will make sure your files are valid, and the second step will ask you to confirm details before processing the log files. The third step will ask you for study metadata as the system processes your files.
